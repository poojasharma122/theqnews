About Us

Where Canada's Game Lives

Trending Now

The Q News — your home for the freshest, fiercest, and most authentic coverage of hockey across Canada and beyond.

Founded by fans, for fans, The Q News was created with one mission: to deliver uncompromising, high-quality hockey news, stories, and insights that speak directly to the heart of the sport. From breaking trades and NHL power rankings to junior prospects and Olympic showdowns, we cover the rink from every angle.

100% Canadian. 100% Hockey.

We’re proud to be a Canadian-owned and operated publication rooted in the deep tradition of our national game. Whether you’re in Toronto, Vancouver, Halifax, or somewhere in between, we’re here to connect you with what’s happening in the sport you love — locally, nationally, and globally.

What We Cover

  • NHL Headlines: Trades, signings, injuries & nightly recaps

  • Fantasy Hockey: Rankings, waiver wire tips, and expert strategy

  • Prospects & Junior Leagues: QMJHL, WHL, OHL coverage and draft watch

  • International Hockey: Olympic previews, World Juniors, and more

  • Opinion & Features: In-depth analysis, editorials, and player spotlights

Digital-First, Fan-Focused

We’ve built The Q News in the style of modern sports coverage—sleek, fast, mobile-friendly, and always up to date. Inspired by outlets like SportalNow, we combine a sharp visual experience with expert hockey writing you can trust.
