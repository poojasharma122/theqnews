Below are the Top 5 Free Fantasy Sports Providers offering hockey leagues in 2025–26, handpicked for their features, reliability, and community.

Top 5 Free Fantasy Sports Providers for Hockey (2025– 26)

1. Fantrax

Website: fantrax.com

Why We Love It

  • Deepest customization of any fantasy platform

  • Dynasty, keeper, and salary cap support

  • Custom scoring, draft pick trading, and minors systems

Perfect For: Hardcore fantasy managers who want full control

2. Yahoo Fantasy Sports

Website: sports.yahoo.com/fantasy

Why We Love It

  • Clean interface and mobile-friendly

  • Automated rankings and trade analyzer

  • Great for casual and competitive leagues alike

Perfect For: Beginners or office leagues that want ease-of-use

3. ESPN Fantasy Hockey

Website: espn.com/fantasy

Why We Love It

  • Integrated player analysis and news from ESPN experts

  • Live scoring and in-depth stats

  • Big league communities and solid app support

Perfect For: Fans who also follow football or basketball and want it all in one place

4. Sleeper (Beta for Hockey)

Website: sleeper.app

Why We Love It

  • Game-changing UI and real-time chat features

  • Already massive for football and growing fast in hockey

  • Built for the next generation of fantasy sports

Perfect For: Younger, mobile-first users and group leagues with banter

5. OfficePools

Website: officepools.com

Why We Love It

  • Longtime favorite in Canadian fantasy pools

  • Easy for playoff drafts, box pools, and season-long

  • Simple interface with a loyal user base

Perfect For: Canadian users or small competitive groups
